Surgery
 

Podiatrists bring a range of skills for treating foot problems, one of which is surgery. We have over 21 years of surgical experience with high patient satisfaction. Our practice will apply conservative methods first to relieve pain and correct a problem. But sometimes conservative care needs adjunctive surgical intervention. We are here to discuss all options to provide the best integrated treatment course based on our experience and results.

We provide in office x-rays convenient to all patients for complete and accurate diagnostic consultation of bone and joint problems. Surgical considerations are then presented, dicussing treatment goals as well as possible complications. Helping you make an educated decision is very important to us. Some surgical procedures are less complicated with low risks and can easily be performed in an office setting. Examples would be nail surgery, removal of warts, biopsies of the skin and nail, and minor corrective toe surgeries. We have full surgical privileges at multiple sugary centers to perform all bone procedures including painful bunions, hammertoes, heel spurs, neuromas and a list too numerous to delineate.

Dr. Ouska-Griffin performs all of her own surgeries and follow up appointments for the best quality care from the pre operative consultation until the patient is fully healed.
